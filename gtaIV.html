Grand Theft Auto: IV



Grand Theft Auto 4 is an open world action game. Similar to GTA: San Andreas, The game is played from a third-person perspective, and its world is navigated on foot or by vehicle and set in the fictional city known as “Liberty City”. You will play as the character ‘Niko Bellic’ who came to Liberty City to live the American dream but gets stopped by mob bosses and has many tasks and corners to get through. You have the ability to ride and steal jets. boats, cars and pick between playing in first or third person.

System Requirements

  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 1.8GHz, AMD Athlon X2 64 2.4GHz
  • Memory: 1.5 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 256MB Nvidia 7900 / 256MB ATI X1900
  • DirectX: Version 9.0
  • Storage: 7 GB available space
