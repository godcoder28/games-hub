Counter Srike: Global Offensive



Overview

Counter Strike Global Offensive is an interactive FPS action game and one of the most known games to date. In this game you are a Terrorist or Counter-Terrorist who has never fired a gun in their life before the round starts. You must help your character learn how to control their guns while killing enemies. Overall, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a surprisingly awesome game about people with the worst gun handling abilities ever seen on Earth. Even though your characters have the aiming abilities of someone you have many tactical options that make the game very fun and enjoyble.

System Requirements

  • OS: Windows® 7/Vista/XP
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ 2 Duo E6600 or AMD Phenom™ X3 8750 processor or better
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Video card must be 256 MB or more and should be a DirectX 9-compatible with support for Pixel Shader 3.0
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 4 GB available space
